   function ema(candles, period):

     return ema

   Calculate TR (true range) for each candle
   Calculate +DM and -DM

   +DI = 100 * smoothed+DM / smoothedTR
   -DI = 100 * smoothed-DM / smoothedTR
   DX = 100 * |+DI - -DI| / (+DI + -DI)

   middle = 20-period SMA of close
   std = standard deviation of last 20 closes
   upper = middle + (2 * std)
   lower = middle - (2 * std)
   width = (upper - lower) / middle * 100

   Calculate average gain and average loss

   RS = avgGain / avgLoss

   highest_high = max of last 5 highs
   lowest_low = min of last 5 lows
   %K = (close - lowest_low) /
