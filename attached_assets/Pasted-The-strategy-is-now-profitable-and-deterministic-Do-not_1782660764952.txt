Current: Move stop to break even when profit 
reaches 1.0× stop distance.

Problem: V75 frequently retraces exactly back 
to entry after moving 1× stop distance before 
continuing. This triggers break even stop 
unnecessarily then price continues to TP2.

Fix: Move stop to break even only when profit
reaches 1.5× stop distance instead of 1.0×.

Also add a break even buffer:
Instead of moving stop exactly to entry price,
move it to entry + 5 pips (for BUY)
or entry - 5 pips (for SELL).
This small buffer prevents the natural 
bid/ask spread from stopping the trade out
exactly at break even.

Current code:
if profitPips >= stopDistance:
  stopLoss = entryPrice

New code:
const BUFFER_PIPS = 5
if profitPips >= stopDistance * 1.5:
  if direction === 'BUY':
    stopLoss = entryPrice + BUFFER_PIPS
  else:
    stopLoss = entryPrice - BUFFER_PIPS
  breakEvenMoved = true
